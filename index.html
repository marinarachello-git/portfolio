What I am
good at

Digital Product Design

End-to-end product work: research, ideation and strategy, design and prototyping, testing and validation, handoff and development— from initial vision through to production-ready outcomes.

Visual Design

A deep craft practice built across brand, product, and systems, with an uncompromising point of view on what good looks like at every scale.

Design Systems

Building systems that last. Translating complex workflows into clear, consistent experiences that scale across platforms and teams.

Strategic Thinking

Framing problems before solving them. Working upstream with clients to shape product direction, define vision, and translate business ambitions into tangible design outcomes.

Research & Discovery

Getting to the right question before reaching for solutions. Planning and running user research to understand behaviour, uncover needs, and turn insight into sketches, flows, and better-informed design decisions.

Client Leadership

Building trusted relationships with senior stakeholders across global organisations — communicating design rationale clearly, navigating trade-offs with confidence, and keeping quality intact under pressure.

AI-Assisted Design Practice

Integrating tools like Claude, Cursor, and Figma Make into real workflows — experimenting with AI to explore ideas faster, prototype and animate new concepts, and evolve how products are designed and built.
